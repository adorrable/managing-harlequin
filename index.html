About Ubuntu Travel & Tours

Ubuntu Travel & Tours is a proudly South African company, based in Johannesburg, passionate about connecting travelers to culture, adventure, and comfort. Operating since 2016, we offer personalized travel planning, group tours, and visa assistance. We design experiences that celebrate Africa and the world with trusted local expertise and global reach.

We tailor itineraries to your interests—safaris, beach escapes, city breaks, and bespoke group adventures—ensuring every journey feels like a personal story rather than a checklist. Our Visa Assistance team helps you navigate requirements for most destinations, making travel simpler and more enjoyable.
